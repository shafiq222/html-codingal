Facts about dogs

Dogs, often called "man's best friend," are domesticated mammals belonging to the Canis lupus familiaris species. They are known for their loyalty, companionship, and diverse range of breeds, each with unique characteristics. Dogs are descendants of wolves and were domesticated thousands of years ago, likely for hunting, guarding, and herding."by Azaan"
