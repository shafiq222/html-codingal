What is skate boarding?

What is skate boarding?

Skateboarding is a sport and recreational activity that involves riding and performing tricks on a skateboard.Riders stand on a flat board with four wheels, using a combination of balance and footwork to propel themselves and execute maneuvers like jumps, flips, and grinds.

What is cricket?

What is cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball team sport played between two teams of 11 players. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team by hitting a ball with a bat and running between wickets, or by hitting the ball to the boundary of the field. The game originated in England and is popular in many countries, especially those in the former British Empire.

What is football?

What is football?

In association football, two teams of 11 players primarily use their feet to move a spherical ball into the opponent's net. In American football, two teams of 11 players advance a prolate spheroid-shaped ball down the field using a combination of running, passing, and kicking plays.

What is basketball?

What is basketball?

Basketball is a team sport played on a rectangular court between two teams of five players each, who score by shooting a ball through a hoop 10 feet high.

What is swimming?

What is swimming?

Swimming is the process of propelling one's body through water using combined arm and leg motions for purposes like recreation, exercise, competition, or survival.
